Participant Information & Consent

Shopping & Product Preferences Study

This study explores how people pay attention to different parts of product pages when shopping online. It is part of a dissertation research project at Bournemouth University.

Before you begin, please read the following:

  • Participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time before submitting.
  • The study takes approximately 12–14 minutes to complete.
  • This study uses your webcam to estimate where you look on screen. No video is recorded — only gaze coordinates. You will be asked to grant camera access on the next screen.
  • No personally identifiable information is collected. All responses are fully anonymous.
  • Data is stored securely in line with GDPR requirements and used for academic research only.

Eligibility: You must be aged 30–45, reside in the United States, shop online at least once a month, and have no diagnosed colour vision deficiency. Please use a laptop or desktop with a working webcam.
