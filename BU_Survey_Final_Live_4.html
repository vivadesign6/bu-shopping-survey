Participant Information & Consent

Shopping & Product Preferences Survey

This study explores how people pay attention to different parts of product pages when shopping online. It is part of a dissertation research project at Bournemouth University.

Before you begin, please read the following:

  • Participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time before submitting by closing your browser. Once submitted, responses cannot be withdrawn as they are fully anonymous and cannot be individually identified.
  • The survey takes approximately 12–15 minutes to complete.
  • No personally identifiable information is collected. Responses are fully anonymous.
  • Data is stored securely via a private Google Sheet accessible only to the researcher, in line with GDPR requirements.
  • Results will be used for academic research only and may be published in aggregate form.
  • This study has received ethical approval from the BU Faculty of Management Ethics Committee.

Eligibility criteria: You must be aged 30–45, reside in the United States, shop online at least once a month, and have no diagnosed colour vision deficiency.
